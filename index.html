Welcome to EnergyWise

Explore how appliance energy consumption impacts bills and the environment. In Australia, televisions are labelled with a star rating under the MEPS program to help compare efficiency.

What is a Star Rating?

The more stars, the more energy-efficient the model is compared to similar products. Star Ratings help you estimate running costs and environmental impact.

Energy Tips

  • Use energy-saving modes and adjust brightness sensibly.
  • Prefer models with higher Star Rating Index (SRI) for long-term savings.
  • Turn devices fully off instead of leaving on standby when not in use.

Did You Know?

Screen size, panel tech, and settings influence TV power draw. Two TVs of the same size can use different amounts of energy depending on efficiency and calibration.
